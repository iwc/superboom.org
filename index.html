Bio

Irwin Coleman is a software developer living in Atlanta, Georgia with over a decade of development experience.

He graduated from the Georgia Institute of Technology with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science in 1996, and later earned his Master of Science in Computer Science in 1999. His studies focused on user interface software, ubiquitous computing, computer graphics, computer vision and networking.

Irwin has developed user interface software for over a decade, for companies such as Nortel Networks, ESPN, AT&T and Yahoo!.

Most recently, he has ventured into rich web user interface, Mac OS X, iPhone software development.

Irwin is happily married to his wife Maribeth and has three cats (and a funny little dog). When not working, Irwin likes running, books, music and movies.
